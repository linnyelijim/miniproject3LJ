FAQ

Go to our contact page. There you will find a form that you can fill out with your name, your e-mail, the subject of your message, and a short description of the or concern. We will reach out to you in approximately 48 hours. Make sure to check your inbox and spam folder.

Click on the BRAINS&GAMES title. Once on the main blog page, you should be able to see all of your posts and the other user's posts. So long as you are signed in to your account, you should see an 'Edit' button located in the top right corner of your post. Click, 'Edit', then scroll to the bottom of the screen. There, you should see a button to delete your account.

Click on the BRAINS&GAMES title. First, ensure that you are logged into your account, otherwise you won't be able to post. Once logged in, follow your screen to the right of the word "Post" and you should see 'New'. Click 'New' and fill out the "Title" and "Body" section of your post. Finally, click save and your post will have posted. Return to the main page and see your post!
